About Focify

Focify is a platform specifically designed to provide an environment that aids in concentration. It allows you to tailor your work habits and schedule breaks as needed. It consists of extremely efficient timers which help us isolate ourselves from distractions and only hold our goal to the supreme. Furthermore, it also has reminders which help organize the user's schedule. The creation of this app has been to help students, employees and everyone in general. We hope we have been successful in this endeavor.



About Us

We hold the ideal of working hard with utmost concentration and perseverance. In order to achieve this goal, we have chosen this concept to base our app upon.
